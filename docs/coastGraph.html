Units in the Gallipoli Diaries, by frequency

This graph takes the location elements that have an attribute value of 'coast' and plots them by frequency throughout the entire Gallipoli Diaries. The most that were mentioned were Suvla Bay (197) and Cape Helles (183). Those were both important since Suvla Bay was the main landing site for the campaign, and Cape Helles was another major landing site for the ground forces.
