Coastal Locations in the Gallipoli Diaries, by frequency

Summary:

This graph takes the location elements that have an attribute value of 'coast' and plots them by frequency throughout the entire Gallipoli Diaries. The coasts were important during the Gallipoli Campaign esepcally for the Allied forces for troop landings, ship movements, and geography for attack plans for the naval forces.
